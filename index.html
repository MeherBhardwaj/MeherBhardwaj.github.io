About

I'm a Machine Learning and NLP researcher working at the intersection of natural language generation and software engineering analytics. I currently work as an Analyst at Deloitte US‑India, where I also explore applications of generative AI in search, automation, and knowledge retrieval. My research has been published in CORE A conferences and a Q1 journal, spanning text generation, semantic parsing, and AI for software engineering.

I graduated with a B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering from the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Senapati, Manipur in May 2024, where I was mentored by Dr. Dennis Singh Moirangthem. My research journey also includes a research internship at the BDA Lab, IIIT Allahabad, under Dr. Sonali Agarwal, and a voluntary research assistantship at the Georgia Institute of Technology.
